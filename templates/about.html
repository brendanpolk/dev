About

Brendan Polk is a graphic and web designer living in Houston, Texas who enjoys mouse-clicking and picture-making. He has worked with clients such as the Discovery Channel, West Coast Choppers, Circuit of the Americas, and the International Rescue Committee. Brendan is also a co-founder of the Lunch Buddies artist collective.
